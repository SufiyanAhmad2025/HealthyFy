Basal Metabolic Rate(BMR)

It’s the minimum number of calories your body needs to stay alive and perform basic functions at complete rest.

These Function Include

  • Breathing
  • Blood circulation
  • Maintaining body temperature
  • Cell production
  • Organ functioning
